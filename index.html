How to play:


Welcome visitor! If you are a long-time gamer than you should be familiar with the infamous purple dragon "Spyro"! The series first hit the gaming market in 1996 and was a best seller. Those who are or aren't familiar with the traditional game, you must collect the gems or crystals in order to level up throughout the game. In this game we made it simple for you. A random number will be generated and you have to select the right sequence of jewels to equal the random number. Remember the jewel values change randomly too! You will have Chose from them to win. This basic test game may seem simple but be careful it is very challenging.
